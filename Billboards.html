Amazing Arduino Animations - A Clinic for Model Railroaders
Dave Ackmann
Gateway Division, NMRA
 
This clinic instructs the viewer on how to make custom billboards, as implemented on the Baden, Vogy & DeSmet HO scale model railroad. It was first presented at the Gateway Division of the NMRA (St Louis), and subsequently at Trainfest 2019.
 
Each part is a video, ranging from 3 minutes to 18 minutes. I hopy you find it useful and enjoyable..
